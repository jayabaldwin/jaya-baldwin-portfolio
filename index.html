About

Hi... my name is Jaya.

I am 23 years old and from Byron Bay.

A few years ago I dabbled in web design, which I loved. However, it's what's underneath the design, that most people are hardly aware of, that fascinates me the most. The “how?” of it all. So much is happening that we can’t see, and to understand all the complexities, be able to apply that to my work and acquire the skills to build limitless applications is end game.

Anyways, outside of all that, I love spending time with my boyfriend and family, being an overly competitive board game participant and trying to stay upright on my roller skates. Just getting outdoors and going for walks makes me extremely happy… not that I’ve been seeing much of the outside world since the commencement of this course haha. At this point I’m just incredibly thankful for investing in a comfy chair.

I’m excited to keep learning, every day, in the ever-changing and evolving tech field.
